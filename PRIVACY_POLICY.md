# Privacy Policy for Flap & Fly

**Last updated: June 29, 2026**

Flap & Fly is developed and published by **[DEVELOPER NAME]**. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Flap & Fly mobile game.

## Information We Collect

Flap & Fly does not directly collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, camera data, or microphone data.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The game stores certain information locally on your device, including:

- High scores and game progress
- Coin balance and purchased game items
- Sound and gameplay settings

This information is not uploaded to a developer-controlled server. It remains on your device and can be removed by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the game.

## In-App Purchases

Flap & Fly offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play Billing. Payments are processed securely by Google Play.

We do not receive or store your payment card or banking information. Google Play may provide the app with purchase-related information, such as the product purchased and its purchase status, so that purchased items can be delivered.

Google's handling of information is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Permissions

The app may use:

- **Internet access** to communicate with Google Play for in-app purchases and related services.
- **Vibration access** to provide gameplay feedback.

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or precise location.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information or share it with advertisers. Information required for in-app purchases may be processed by Google Play according to Google's policies.

## Data Security

Flap & Fly does not operate a server that stores your personal information. Local game data remains on your device, while payment transactions are handled securely by Google Play.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data is retained on your device until you clear the app's data or uninstall the app. Flap & Fly does not provide user accounts and does not retain personal data on a developer-controlled server.

For information about deleting purchase or account-related data held by Google, please refer to your Google Account and Google Play settings.

## Children's Privacy

Flap & Fly does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app's features or legal requirements change. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
